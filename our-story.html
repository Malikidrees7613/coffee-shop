Our Heritage

A Journey of Craft & Community

It Started With a Single Bean

Founded in 2012 by two passionate coffee enthusiasts, Artisan Coffee began as a modest 200-square-foot roastery in the back of an old warehouse. Our singular mission was to bring out the natural, terroir-driven flavors of coffee beans without masking them behind dark, oily roasts.

We spent years traveling to the high-altitude farms of Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, forging direct relationships with the farmers who share our obsession with quality. Today, that direct-trade model remains the beating heart of everything we do.

"Quality is never an accident. It is always the result of intelligent effort."
